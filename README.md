# Solitaire Poker

A fun card game where you cycle through a deck and build poker hands!

## How to Play

1. Click "Start Game" to begin
2. Cards are shown one at a time - place them in one of 5 hand slots or skip
3. Complete 4 rounds with increasing difficulty:
   - Round 1: 2 cards per hand
   - Round 2: 3 cards per hand
   - Round 3: 4 cards per hand
   - Round 4: 5 cards per hand
4. Win by completing all 4 rounds!

## Scoring

Score = Hand Probability Points + Card Strength (sum of card values)
- Jokers are wild and count as 14 (Ace value)
- Higher cards = higher scores
- Rarer hands = more points

Open `index.html` in your browser or visit the live demo!
