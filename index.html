How to Play

Game Overview

Build 5 poker hands per round by cycling through a 54-card deck (52 cards + 2 Jokers). Complete 4 rounds with increasing difficulty to win!

Rounds

  • Round 1: 2 cards per hand (10 cards total) - 1 minute bonus ceiling
  • Round 2: 3 cards per hand (15 cards total) - 2 minute bonus ceiling
  • Round 3: 4 cards per hand (20 cards total) - 4 minute bonus ceiling
  • Round 4: 5 cards per hand (25 cards total) - 8 minute bonus ceiling

Gameplay

  • Cards are shown one at a time
  • Click "Place Here" to add the card to a hand
  • Click "Skip Card" to pass on a card
  • Each hand must meet the minimum score to continue
  • Jokers are wild cards (match any rank/suit)
  • Get a fresh shuffled deck each round

Minimum Scores

  • Round 1: 104 points (pair of 2s)
  • Round 2: 156 points (pair of 2s)
  • Round 3: 208 points (pair of 2s)
  • Round 4: 260 points (pair of 2s)

Scoring Formula

Hand Score = Base Points + Card Strength

Card Strength = Sum of all card values (2-10 = face value, J=11, Q=12, K=13, A=14, Joker=0)
